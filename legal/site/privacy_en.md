## 1. Introduction and Operator Identity

This Privacy Policy explains how matkon ("the Service", "we") collects, uses, shares and protects your personal information when you use the application and the website. It forms an integral part of the Terms of Use and should be read together with them. By using the Service you confirm that you have read and understood this policy.

**The operator.** The Service is currently operated by an individual ("the operator"), located in New Zealand. No legal entity has yet been registered; once one is, its rights and obligations under this policy will be transferred to it (see the assignment clause in the Terms of Use). The operator is the "agency" for the purposes of the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, and the "controller" for the purposes of the GDPR, to the extent it applies.

**Privacy contact.** For any question, request or complaint relating to privacy, contact us at privacy@matkon.co. We will endeavour to respond within a reasonable time, and in any event within the periods set by applicable law.

## 2. What Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

**(a) Account information you provide at registration.** Email address, password (stored encrypted with our identity provider and not accessible to us in plain text), first and last name, and country of residence. You may later add a personal description (bio).

**(b) Information from third-party sign-in (SSO).** If you choose to sign in with a Google or Apple account, we receive from the provider an account identifier, email address and the name associated with the account, in accordance with the permissions you approve with the provider.

**(c) Content you create in the Service.** Recipes, preparation steps, ingredients, images (that you uploaded or that were generated for you using AI), comments, likes, saved recipes, and sharing actions. Your shopping list is stored in your browser and is not sent to us.

**(d) Technical information collected automatically.** During certain actions, including acceptance of the Terms of Use, we record your IP address, browser and device type (user-agent), and timestamps. This information is collected for security, abuse prevention, and to document your acceptance of the Terms. Our hosting servers also record technical access data routinely.

**(e) Usage and moderation information.** We record usage data of AI-based features (for operational purposes and cost control) and records relating to enforcement of the content rules.

**What we do not collect.** We do not collect your precise address, GPS location, or payment details (the Service is provided free of charge).

## 3. How We Collect Information

We collect information in three ways:

**(a) Directly from you.** When you register, complete your profile, create or upload content, and interact with the Service.

**(b) Automatically.** When you use the Service, we and our hosting servers automatically collect certain technical information (such as IP address, browser and device type, and timestamps), as detailed in Section 2. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies; information is stored on your device using local storage (localStorage) for operational purposes only, such as keeping you signed in and storing your shopping list.

**(c) From third parties.** If you choose to sign in with Google or Apple, we receive basic information about your account from them in accordance with the permissions you approved, as detailed in Section 2.

## 4. Why We Use Information and the Legal Basis

We use your information for the following purposes:

**(a) Operating the Service and providing features.** Managing your account, publishing and displaying recipes, powering the community, saving, comments, likes and sharing. Legal basis: performance of our contract with you (GDPR Art 6(1)(b)).

**(b) AI-based features.** Processing and organising recipes, translating ingredients, assisting in locating rare ingredients, and generating images, as detailed in Section 12. Legal basis: performance of the contract and our legitimate interest in providing the Service (Art 6(1)(b), (f)).

**(c) Security, abuse prevention and enforcement.** Detecting and blocking spam and abusive behaviour, rate limiting, cost control, and content moderation. Legal basis: our legitimate interest in protecting the Service and its users (Art 6(1)(f)).

**(d) Legal compliance and documenting consent.** Retaining a record of your acceptance of the Terms (including IP address and timestamp), and responding to legal and regulatory requests. Legal basis: legal obligation and legitimate interest (Art 6(1)(c), (f)).

**(e) Operational communications.** Sending account-related messages, such as email verification and password reset. Legal basis: performance of the contract (Art 6(1)(b)).

If we wish to use information for a new purpose that is incompatible with these purposes, we will seek your consent or update this policy in advance.

Wherever required, we take reasonable measures to ensure that the transfer is subject to appropriate safeguards, for example through providers subject to recognised data protection frameworks or standard transfer agreements. ## 7. Cookies and Storage Technologies

We do not use tracking, advertising, or third-party analytics cookies. The Service does not track you across websites and does not share information with advertising networks.

To operate the Service we use local storage in your browser (localStorage), for essential purposes only: keeping you signed in so you do not have to sign in each time, storing your shopping list in your browser, and storing local display preferences. This information is stored in the browser you are using, is not synced to other devices, and is not sent to us. You may delete it at any time via your browser settings (deleting it may sign you out and reset local preferences).

Our sign-in and infrastructure providers (see Section 5) may use their own technical storage mechanisms required for security and for the functioning of the Service.

## 8. How Long We Retain Information

We retain personal information only for as long as it is needed for the purposes for which it was collected:

**(a) Account information and content.** Retained for as long as your account is active. Upon deletion of the account, we will remove your profile and content, subject to the exceptions in the license clause of the Terms of Use: reasonable backup copies for a limited time, and content you shared in the community that other users have already saved.

**(b) Terms acceptance logs (including IP address).** Retained for a reasonable period to document your acceptance, for security, and for defence against claims, including after deletion of the account, to the extent required by law or to protect a legal right.

**(c) Technical server logs and moderation records.** Retained for short periods in accordance with our infrastructure providers' policies and for security and enforcement purposes.

**(d) Legal obligations.** In certain cases we may be required to retain information for a longer period if the law so requires.

After the retention period ends, we will delete the information or de-identify it, to the extent reasonably possible.

## 10. Information Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, use, alteration or disclosure. Among others:

- **Encryption in transit.** Communication with the Service takes place over an encrypted connection (HTTPS).
- **Data-level access separation.** Authorisation mechanisms in the database ensure that each user accesses only their own information, unless the content was shared publicly.
- **Password protection.** Passwords are stored encrypted with the identity provider and are not accessible to us in plain text. We also support sign-in using passkeys.
- **Abuse controls.** Rate limiting, moderation, and enforcement against violating accounts.

**Who accesses information.** Besides you, only the operator and authorised personnel may access information to the extent needed to operate the Service, for support and for moderation. Access is limited and logged.

Please note: no security method is perfect, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your sign-in details confidential. If a security incident occurs that requires it, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by law.

## 11. Children's Information

If we learn that information was collected from a minor under the permitted age without an appropriate basis, we will delete the information and the account within a reasonable time.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe a minor has provided us with information, contact us at privacy@matkon.co and we will address it.

In addition, and as detailed in the Terms of Use, images of children under the age of 18 may not be uploaded without the consent of a parent or guardian.

## 12. AI Content Processing

Some features of the Service rely on artificial intelligence providers (see Section 5) for processing and organising recipes, translating ingredients, assisting in locating rare ingredients, and content review (moderation).

**What is sent for processing.** For these features we send the recipe content you create or upload: the recipe text, the ingredients, and images. To locate rare ingredients we also send your country of residence (to tailor the search), but not your precise address. To generate a recipe image we send only a textual description of the dish.

**What we do not send.** We do not send the AI providers your email address, password, full name, or personal description (bio). AI processing focuses on the recipe content, not on your identity.

**Accuracy.** Content generated or processed using AI may contain inaccuracies, and images generated using AI do not necessarily reflect the actual dish. Do not rely on it as professional advice (see also the Terms of Use and the food safety clause).
